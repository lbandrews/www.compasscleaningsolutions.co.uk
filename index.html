About Compass Cleaning Solutions

Brendan Hawkins

Managing Director

Formed in March of 2012, Compass Cleaning Solutions Limited was the entrepreneurial development by Brendan Hawkins. Since inception five years ago, Compass Cleaning Solutions LTD became a market leader in the West Yorkshire area and his since expanded to operate all across the UK. The recipe for Compass' success is a combination of 25+ years of experience in the cleaning industry, exceptional management of B2B Relationships and family friendly traditional values.
